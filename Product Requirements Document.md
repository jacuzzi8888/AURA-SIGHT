# **PRD: Aura Sight – Multimodal Assistive Pathfinder**

## **1\. Vision & Purpose**

Aura Sight is a "Visual Proxy" for the visually impaired. It leverages Gemini’s native multimodal capabilities to provide real-time, low-latency spatial awareness, safety monitoring, and social context. The goal is to maximize the user's quality of life and independence through a single, voice-driven interface.

## **2\. Core Features (The "Core 4")**

1. **The Pathfinder (Spatial Navigation):** Real-time scanning of the user's immediate environment to identify floor-level and chest-high obstructions.  
2. **The Guardian (Health & Safety):** Intelligent identification of allergens, medication labels, and expiration dates with proactive warning logic.  
3. **The Social Mirror (Dignity & Style):** Contextual description of fashion (color/pattern matching) and social cues (detecting facial expressions and moods of people nearby).

4. #### **Light & Energy Sense (Household Utility)**

   * **What it does:** Detects if lights or appliances (like a gas stove) are left on. A crucial safety feature for living independently.

## **3\. User Experience (UX) Design**

* **One-Button Interface:** The entire mobile screen acts as a haptic-responsive button. Hold to stream/talk, release to pause.  
* **Active Perception (The Director):** The agent proactively instructs the user on how to position the camera for better accuracy (e.g., "Tilt up," "Move back").  
* **Voice-First Output:** All feedback is delivered via high-quality, low-latency audio.

## **4\. Competitive Advantage for Hackathon**

* **Multimodal Live API Integration:** Near-zero latency compared to standard REST-based AI apps.  
* **Proactive Intervention:** The AI doesn't just answer; it warns (Safety-First).  
* **Agentic Corrections:** The AI fixes the user's framing, showing it understands the "goal" of the interaction.

## **5\. Success Metrics**

* **Latency:** \<500ms from vision capture to audio feedback.  
* **Accuracy:** \>90% identification of common trip hazards and allergens.  
* **User Stickiness:** Ability to complete a task (e.g., checking a bill) in under 30 seconds.
